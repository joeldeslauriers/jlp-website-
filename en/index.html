Google Drive Guide — Step by Step

  1. Go to drive.google.com and create a new folder named after your song
  2. Upload all your WAV files into that folder
  3. Right-click the folder → Share → Copy link
  4. In sharing settings, change access to "Anyone with the link" can view
  5. Paste the link in the Google Drive Link field of the form below
